Privacy Policy

Emailix - A Nebulix Product - Last updated: 11 May 2026 - Effective: 11 May 2026
Emailix lets users build and preview emailers for free. ZIP downloads are paid and require an active Pro or Team license key. Normal editing stays in your browser where possible.

01 Who We Are

Emailix is an online email template builder operated by Nebulix (nebulixcloud.com), built and maintained by Liju Pankajakshan, based in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates.

For privacy questions, contact hello@nebulixcloud.com.

03 Browser Storage

Emailix uses browser localStorage to keep your draft and active download license available between sessions. You can clear this data through your browser settings at any time.

  • emailix-draft stores your saved draft structure and global settings
  • emailix-license stores the license key used to unlock downloads
  • emailix-license-info stores plan and expiration metadata returned after activation

04 Paid Downloads

Building and previewing emailers is free. Downloading ZIP packages requires an active Emailix license. Current plans are Emailix Pro at USD 15 per month and Emailix Team at USD 39 per month.

Payment processing may be handled by a third-party provider when checkout is connected. That provider will process payment details under its own privacy policy.

05 Screenshot Deterrents

Emailix includes browser-level protections that block right-click, copy, print, drag, and some screenshot keyboard shortcuts where the browser exposes those events. Operating systems and browsers may still allow screen capture that a web app cannot detect or prevent.
